About Bahir Dar

Discover the heart and soul of our remarkable city

Welcome to Bahir Dar

Bahir Dar, the capital of the Amhara Region, is a vibrant city located on the southern shore of Lake Tana, the source of the Blue Nile. Known for its wide avenues lined with palm trees and colorful flowers, Bahir Dar is one of Ethiopia's leading tourist destinations.

The city is famous for its ancient monasteries, stunning waterfalls, and vibrant local culture. Whether you're exploring the historic monasteries on Lake Tana, visiting the majestic Blue Nile Falls, or experiencing the bustling local markets, Bahir Dar offers something for everyone.

January 2025 Urban Development

Lake Tana Ecosystem Conservation Program

February 2025 Environmental Protection

The Bahir Dar Environmental Protection Agency has intensified efforts to preserve and restore the unique ecosystem of Lake Tana, a critical biodiversity hotspot.

Conservation Efforts:

  • Biodiversity Monitoring Program
  • Sustainable Fishing Regulations
  • Community-Based Conservation Initiatives
  • Watershed Restoration Projects

Our commitment to environmental stewardship ensures the protection of Lake Tana's rich ecological heritage for future generations.
